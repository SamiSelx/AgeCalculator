########## The Age Is Approximate ########## 

Calculation Method :

1- calcul the difference Between current Date And Date Of Birth (MilliSecond);
2- convert Result to year by Devide 1000 / 60 / 60 / 24 / 365
3- Get Numbers Of Days by calcul mod 365
4- Get Months By Devide Numbers Of Days By 31
5- Get The Remaining Days by calcul mod 31 (rest of division)

########## Note ########## 
This result is an approximation since not every month or year has 31 or 365 days respectively.
